## **ECE 2040** - Circuit Analysis
Analysis of linear circuits using time-domain and frequency-domain techniques, including AC/DC analysis, phasors, and network theorems.

## **ECE 2026** - Introduction to Signal Processing
Fundamentals of digital signal processing, including discrete-time signals, Fourier transforms, filtering, and signal analysis techniques.

## **ECE 2035** - Programming for Hardware/Software Systems
Programming fundamentals for embedded systems, covering C programming, data structures, and hardware-software interfaces.

## **ECE 2020** - Digital System Design
Introduction to digital logic design, combinational and sequential circuits, and digital system implementation.

## **ECE 3040** - Microelectronic Circuits *(In Progress)*
Analysis and design of analog electronic circuits including amplifiers, oscillators, and filters.

## **ECE 3025** - Electromagnetics *(In Progress)*
Electromagnetic field theory, wave propagation, and transmission lines.

## **Silicon Jackets** - Analog CMOS IC Design Lectures
Lectures covering analog mixed-signal design fundamentals and MOSFET architecture/physics with hands-on projects including digital inverters, single-stage amplifiers, and ring oscillators in Cadence Virtuoso. Content based on ECE 3400 textbook.
