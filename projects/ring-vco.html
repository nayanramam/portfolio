Analog IC Design

Ring VCO Design + Layout in Cadence Virtuoso

Synopsis

Designed a current-starved ring-oscillator VCO in Cadence Virtuoso (Sky130 PDK), enabling voltage-controlled frequency tuning with fewer stages than a standard inverter chain. Verified performance across ff/tt/ss corners and 0-70 °C, and added an inverter buffer to drive a 1 pF load while staying within the power spec.
